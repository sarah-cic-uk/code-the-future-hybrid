AI Session - Lesson 5

Review and repeat

The strongest AI workflow is: build, check, improve, and talk about the result. AI should make review easier, not replace it.

This final lesson is about building confidence. First you prove that you can do the work yourself, then you use AI to speed up or sharpen your result, and finally you explain what changed.

Congratulations on Completing AI Session!

You now understand how to use AI as a tool while maintaining your skills and professional value.

The Healthy AI Workflow

Process Four-step approach

  1. Try it yourself first - Build your understanding and baseline
  2. Use AI to compare, explain, or improve - Get alternative perspectives
  3. Test the result - Verify everything works as expected
  4. Talk through the final choice with a human - Get real feedback

Final Activity

Practice Revisit an earlier section

  1. Choose an earlier lesson from the course
  2. Rebuild one small part on your own
  3. Ask AI how to improve it
  4. Compare the two versions
  5. Keep the one that is clearest to you

Examples: HTML structure, headings, links, or a simple CSS layout.

Checklist Human review comes first

  • Check that the code still works - Test functionality
  • Explain the changes in your own words - Ensure understanding
  • Ask another person for feedback - Get external perspective
  • Only finish when it makes sense - To a human reader
