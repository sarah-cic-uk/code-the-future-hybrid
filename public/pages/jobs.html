Tech Entry @ IBM

Are you ex-military and ready for a career change that can take you to new heights in the dynamic world of technology?

The IBM CIC are introducing an exciting career opportunity to join our Software Engineering Practice via the Tech Entry Scheme

We are thrilled to offer this exceptional opportunity for individuals transitioning into IT and software development (even without a formal degree). At IBM CIC we believe that passion, dedication, and a willingness to learn are the driving forces being success in this ever-evolving industry. We understand that some of the most talented individuals may not have pursued the traditional educational paths in IT or may have done and have IT industry experience but are in a position that may not be rewarding enough and that's why we are committed to providing a comprehensive training pathway to kick-start your career.

Why consider a career in IT and software development with us?

  • Training will be provided: We strongly believe in investing in our employees' growth and development. Even if you don't have a technical background, we will offer an extensive training program that covers the essential skills and knowledge needed to thrive in IT and software development. This training will initially involve learning both front and backend software languages and Cloud technologies across the following disciplines:
    • Java
    • JavaScript
    • TypeScript
    • Git & GitHub
    • React/Vuejs
    • AWS/Azure
    As your career evolves you will gain further experience across networking, infrastructure, cyber security, AI, and cloud computing. Our team of experienced professionals will guide you every step of the way, ensuring a smooth transition into this field.
  • Diverse and challenging projects: As a member of our team, you will have the opportunity to work on a wide range of projects that span different industries and sectors. From developing cutting edge applications to creating innovative solutions, you will be the forefront of driving technical advancements and making a tangible impact.
  • Collaborative and supportive work environment: We foster a collaborative culture that values teamwork, open communication, and knowledge sharing. You'll be part of a supportive community where your ideas are encouraged, and your growth is nurtured. Our team members come from diverse backgrounds, bringing unique perspectives that enrich our work and create an inclusive atmosphere.
  • Career progression and growth: We are committed to your long-term success. As you build your skills and gain experience, various opportunities for career progression and growth within the company will become available. We believe in recognizing and rewarding talent, providing a clear path for advancement based on your abilities and achievements.

This would be a 6-month Cloud Fullstack Engineer role with the potential to extend into a permanent contract. Your first 6 weeks will cover technical skills development and introduce you to your industry and project team so you are ready to join a Software Engineering team from week 7 onwards

The role will be in a Public Sector account and allow you to utilise your experience and industry knowledge to help problem solve, devise solutions and build new technology to advance industry processes.

Tech Re-Entry @ IBM

The role is suitable for someone who has previously worked within the IT sector and is looking to return after a career break of at least a year.

We are ideally looking for people that have previous experience in one of the following areas:

  • Project Management
  • Business Analysis
  • Front-end Development
  • Back-end Development
  • Full Stack Engineering
  • Quality Engineering (Test Specialists)
  • Technical Support

We understand that re-joining employment after a career break can be nerve-racking and difficult. That is why we have introduced the tech re-entry programme to enable talented individuals like you the opportunity to re-start your career. In your first six-months with the CIC the tech re-entry programme will provide opportunities to update your skills, access to the latest tools and technologies and work alongside multi-disciplinary teams to hone expertise and deliver on client-critical solutions.

So why join the programme?

  • The program offers flexibility and ease of transition from your career break to getting back to the workforce
  • A community of like-minded re-joiners will support you as you restart your journey together
  • You will have the opportunity to update your skills and expertise as you work on real projects with real impact
  • A dedicated mentor will help you get up to speed with the environment and new technology

Experienced Hires @ IBM

Our Experienced roles allow you to work in one of our IBM Consulting Client Innovation Centres (Delivery Centres), where we deliver deep technical and industry expertise to a wide range of public and private sector clients around the world. Our delivery centres offer our clients locally based skills and technical expertise to drive innovation and adoption of new technology.

A career in IBM Consulting is rooted by long-term relationships and close collaboration with clients across the globe.

You'll work with visionaries across multiple industries to improve the hybrid cloud and AI journey for the most innovative and valuable companies in the world. Your ability to accelerate impact and make meaningful change for your clients is enabled by our strategic partner ecosystem and our robust technology platforms across the IBM portfolio, including Software and Red Hat.
