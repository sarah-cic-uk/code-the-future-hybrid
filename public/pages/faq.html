Code the Future is a free coding bootcamp focused on HTML and CSS. It offers structured guidance and personalised support to help individuals learn essential web development skills and prepare for careers in tech.

This online course is designed for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced. Whether you're just starting out or looking to enhance your skills, this course is perfect for you.

In this course, you will learn the fundamentals of web development, as well as advanced techniques and strategies. You will gain a deep understanding of the concepts and develop practical skills that you can apply in real-world scenarios.

The course is self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own convenience. On average, most students complete the course within 6-8 weeks. However, you can take longer if needed or finish it sooner if you dedicate more time to it.

No, there are no prerequisites for this course except access to an available computer system to complete the online coding tasks. It is designed to accommodate students of all levels, including beginners with no prior knowledge of coding.
