Other Opportunities with IBM CIC


Join an IBM Hackathon


IBM CIC run external hackathons for colleges and universities to allow to experience life as an IBMer.

After attending a Hackathon participants will be able to describe the software lifecycle from Problem Statement to working Proof of Concept, and design, create and test a prototype app/site. They will have greater understanding of the role of a Software Engineering in a Consultancy and be able to present ideas and apps to clients in a clear, concise manner.


Pending application for Credly Badge to allow hackathon participants to share their successful completion of an IBM hackathon on linkedIn and CVs to help them demonstrate that they have learnt technical and software lifecycle skills and can implement those skills in a hackathon (and therefore professional) environment.
